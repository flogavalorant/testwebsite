About Me

Selva Chetty is a dedicated Sydney-based mortgage broker, known for his energetic, empathetic, and motivational approach. With a passion for helping clients achieve their financial goals through strategic planning, Selva began his mortgage career in 2016 after five years as a Senior Credit Adviser. His expertise allows him to simplify the complex home loan process for clients. He is committed to providing comprehensive service, from start to finish, with a focus on attention to detail, clear communication, and exceptional customer care. Clients appreciate his confidence, discipline, and insightful approach to securing the best outcomes. Selva believes in continuous improvement and stays updated on industry trends to offer the best possible service. He holds a Cert IV in Finance & Mortgage Broking and is a member of the Finance Brokers Association of Australia (FBAA). Outside of work, Selva enjoys traveling and staying fit through swimming, dancing, and calisthenics.
